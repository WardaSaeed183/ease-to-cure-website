Causes of High Blood Pressure

  • Poor diet, lack of exercise, obesity, smoking, and stress.

Symptoms

  • Headaches, dizziness, chest pain, and vision problems.

Prevention

  • Maintain a healthy lifestyle, exercise regularly, and reduce salt intake.
